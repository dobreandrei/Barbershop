Just you and your barber

Here at the Gentlemen Cut we know that your time is valuable. We want you to get the most out of your visit to our barbershop. There's no waiting around for other clients. It's just you and your barber, who will not only cut your hair but also offer you advice regarding your style.

We do things oldschool

Our styles may be modern but our techniques have been tried and tested by generations of barbers before us. We believe in doing things oldschool and offering you the best customer service experience possible, because being a gentleman is not just about looks: it's a lifestyle.

The best styling products

Razors, pomades, creams, shampoos...you name it, we guarantee it's the best stuff you'll find out there. Barbering isn't just about skill (all though most of it is). The tools of the trade are also important in making you look like a million bucks.
